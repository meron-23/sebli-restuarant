Our Ethiopian Lounge

Our lounge area offers an authentic Ethiopian atmosphere with traditional decor, perfect for enjoying Ethiopian coffee, honey wine (tej), and cultural conversation. We feature traditional Ethiopian beverages including Habesha beer, spiced teas, and our famous coffee ceremony. Whether you're looking to experience Ethiopian culture or enjoy our late-night lounge with hookah and music, our space provides the perfect Ethiopian setting.
