About Us

Welcome to Sebli Ethiopian

Sebli Ethiopian Restaurant & Lounge offers a unique dining experience that brings the rich and diverse flavors of Ethiopian cuisine to Stone Mountain, Georgia. Our family-owned establishment is dedicated to preserving and sharing the heritage of Ethiopian cuisine, known for its high-quality ingredients, authentic spice blends, and welcoming atmosphere.
